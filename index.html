What is AniMagic?

AniMagic is an Adobe Animate extension that reduces the time, cost and learning curve that modern day animators face on a day-to-day basis, making it the perfect assistant to existing animation software.

Gone are the days of suffering from outdated animation tools and struggling with the multi-software workflow. By incorporating the tools and features of both modern 2D and 3D animation suites in one place, AniMagic is tailored to animators of all skill levels and specializations alike.

Beginners will find that the simple, one-software solution reduces the animation learning curve, and exponentially increases their improvement rate. Meanwhile, the productivity of experienced animators will be taken to the next level with Animagic’s 3D redisplay technology.

AniMagic is the tool those eager to learn animation have been waiting for, and the tool experienced animators didn’t know they needed.

If you want to take your animation to the next dimension, you want AniMagic.

3D redisplay technology

AniMagic's 3D redisplay technology automates the redisplay of 3D backgrounds without requiring animators to redraw each frame reducing the cost and time to produce quality animation.

Intuitive Design

AniMagic's animation stuidio offers a simple interface that allows new animators and veteran animators alike to start animating faster and more efficiently than ever before.

One Stop Solution

AniMagic's animation studio is the only software to offer the latest and greatest tools for both 2D and 3D animation in one place.
